# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Car owners in Gravesend, Kent — arriving via Google search, comparing local mechanics. Includes a meaningful proportion of elderly users who need accessible, unhurried interfaces.

## Product Purpose

Generate quote requests and phone calls. The site exists to convert a search into a booking. Every page serves that goal.

## Brand Personality

Reliable, local, clean — the shop you trust your car to without a second thought. Not flashy, not corporate. Neighbourhood expertise.

## Anti-references

None specified.

## Design Principles

1. Trust through clarity — no clutter, no noise; every element should make a first-time visitor feel confident
2. Action over decoration — the site exists to generate calls and quotes; ornament that doesn't serve that is waste
3. Local, not generic — personal and grounded, not a national-chain template
4. Accessible by default — elderly users are a known audience; large targets, high contrast, plain language
5. Green earns its place — the accent is earned by CTAs and key moments, not scattered for decoration

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. Elderly users are a known audience — generous tap targets (min 44×44px), readable type scale, high contrast, plain language throughout.
